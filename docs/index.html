ZoomNote Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ZoomNote is a local note-taking app. Your notes and drawings stay on your device.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. ZoomNote does not transmit any information to external servers. All notes, drawings, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Android's built-in SQLite database.

Data Stored on Your Device

ZoomNote stores your drawings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

ZoomNote does not request any special permissions beyond those required to run (screen display, touch input). It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or network.

Third-Party Services

ZoomNote does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

ZoomNote does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at the email address listed on our Google Play store page.
