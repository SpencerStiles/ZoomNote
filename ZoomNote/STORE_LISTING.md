## Full Description (4000 chars max)

ZoomNote is a canvas that zooms — infinitely.

Zoom in to write small details. Zoom out to see the big picture. Every stroke lives at the zoom level where you drew it, and fades naturally as you move away. Your notes organize themselves by scale.

**How it works**

Draw anywhere on the canvas with your finger or USI stylus. Pinch to zoom in or out. Strokes made at your current zoom level are vivid. As you zoom away from where you drew them, they fade — so your canvas never feels cluttered, no matter how much is on it.

It's a different way to think about notes. Instead of pages, you have depth.

**Features**

- Infinite zoom canvas — zoom from 0.001x to 1,000,000x
- Pressure-sensitive stylus support (USI 2.0 — works with Pixel, Chromebook styluses)
- Finger drawing — draw with your finger, pinch to zoom
- 8 pen colors: black, red, blue, green, orange, purple, gray, white
- 3 stroke widths: fine, medium, bold
- Eraser tool
- Undo / redo (tap toolbar, or two-finger tap / three-finger tap on canvas)
- Dot grid that adapts to your zoom level
- Strokes fade by zoom proximity — your current zoom level is always clean and readable
- Everything saved automatically — your canvas is there when you come back
- Works completely offline — no account, no cloud, no tracking

**Who it's for**

- Students who want to zoom in on a diagram and annotate the details
- Artists who sketch at a broad level then zoom in to refine
- Anyone who thinks in layers and hates running out of space
- Stylus users who want a responsive, low-latency drawing experience

**Stylus support**

ZoomNote works great with USI 2.0 styluses (Samsung S Pen Pro, Penoval USI 2.0, Staedtler Noris digital, and others). Pressure sensitivity gives your strokes natural weight — press harder for thicker lines, lighter for fine details.

**Privacy**

No account required. No data collected. Your drawings stay on your device. Always.
